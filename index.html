About Us

New RK Glass & Plywood Centre is Varanasi's trusted destination for premium interior building materials. We stock a wide range of products across plywood, glass, laminates, hardware, doors and decorative wall solutions — everything under one roof.

Founded with the mission of making high-quality interior materials accessible in Varanasi, we serve homeowners, contractors, architects and interior designers across the region.
